Core & Security Business Developer

Sandstorm + Business

Sandstorm + Business extends Sandstorm with features that businesses need.

On-prem deployment

Get the ease-of-use of SaaS while keeping your data in-house. Deploy to physical machines or any private or public cloud.

LDAP and Active Directory integration

Let employees log in with their company-wide credentials.

Group Management

Use LDAP/AD groups for sharing and access control.

Global Access Control

Set organization-wide access control policies, such as prohibiting your employees from sharing grains outside of the organization.

Global Audit Logging

Monitor data across your whole organization to keep track of who has accessed what.

Sandstorm + Scale

Sandstorm + Scale includes all Sandstorm + Business features and is the on-prem version of the same technology we use to run Sandstorm Oasis.

Automatic Scale-out

Scale out free machines to thousands as your needs grow

Automatic Failover

Random hardware failures are invisible to your users

Automatic Encryption

Every grain is encrypted at rest with a unique key which can only be accessed by those with whom the grain is shared
